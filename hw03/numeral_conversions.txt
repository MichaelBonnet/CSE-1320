Calculating Base 10 From Base 2
***Place Value * 2^Place from the right starting at 0***
1101 = (1 * 2^3) + (1 * 2^2) + (0 * 2^1) + (1 * 2^0)
     = (1 * 8)   + (1 * 4)   + (0 * 2)   + (1 * 1)
     = (8)       + (4)       + (0)       + (1)
     = 13
